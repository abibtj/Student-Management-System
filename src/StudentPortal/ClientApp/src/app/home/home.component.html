Hello, welcome to Student Portal!

This portal allows you manage students in your school. Some of the things you can do include:

  • View all students in a paginated grid format.
  • Add, edit and delete students.
  • Filter / Search for specific students.
  • View student distributions by gender, class, and so on...

To help you get started, you have to:

  • Click on Register link in the menu bar, then register with a valid email address and a secure password.
  • Check you email box and click on the link sent to you in order to confirm your email.
  • Once you confirm your email, you can then login to Student Portal and start to explore.
